Weather

Current weather for a specific location and 15 hours forecast. Uses OpenWeatherMap API.

Source on github

Only Ottawa?

Since that's the city I live in at the moment it is the default. But you can of course get other cities as well, just provide the city in the command.

Possible cmd flags:

  • -c <city name> to get weather for a different city, e.g. Berlin
  • -u <unit name> to see the information in another unit, e.g. kelvin
